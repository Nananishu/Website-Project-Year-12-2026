What is MotoGP?

Controlled Chaos...
at 360 km/h

MotoGP isn't just racing — it's a battle between man and machine at the outer edge of what's physically possible. Riders balance 160kg bikes on a knife-edge, knees grazing asphalt, while navigating corners faster than any road car could manage in a straight line.

The championship spans five continents and twenty-two events each season, drawing the most talented motorcycle racers on the planet. Every fraction of a second is the product of thousands of engineering hours and years of riding mastery.
